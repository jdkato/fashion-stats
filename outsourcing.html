Apparel: By The Numbers

July 8, 2016 by Joseph Kato

Inspired by The Bureau of Labor Statistics' (BLS) Spotlight on Statistics series, we take an updated look at the apparel industry through the lens of statistics. The BLS offers a wealth of data to the public but, due to the sheer quantity, it can be a chore to sift through. So, in the following sections, we aim to present the informative data in a user-friendly way. Enjoy!

Where is apparel made?

Mouseover an individual state to see more details.
