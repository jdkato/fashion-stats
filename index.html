Fashion: By The Numbers

July 3, 2016 by Joseph Kato

Inspired by The Bureau of Labor Statistics' (BLS) Spotlight on Statistics series, we take an updated look at the apparel industry through the lens of statistics. The BLS offers a wealth of data to the public but, due to the sheer quantity, it can be hard to sift through without knowledge of statistical software. So, in the following sections, we aim to present the informative data in a user-friendly way. Enjoy!

Where is apparel made?

Heads up! You can mouseover an individual state to see more details.

Shown above is a 2015 state-by-state breakdown of where apparel manufacturers are located in the U.S. There were a total of 6958 apparel manufacturers in 2015 (as defined by the North American Industry Classification System), which continued a fairly significant downward trend:

Year Manufacturers 5-Year Difference Percentage Change
1990 20786 N/A N/A
1995 21354 568 2.7
2000 16770 -4584 -21.5
2005 11258 -5512 -32.9
2010 7855 -3403 -30.2
2015 6958 -897 -11.4

One possible explanation for this is the ever-increasing role outsourcing plays in U.S. manufacturing.
