Apparel: By The Numbers

July 8, 2016 by Joseph Kato

Inspired by The Bureau of Labor Statistics' (BLS) Spotlight on Statistics series, we will explore data from the Consumer Expenditure Survey with the goal of gaining a better understanding of apparel spending habbits in the U.S. We will then use this understanding to dicuss strategies for reducing our overall investment into the fashion industry, which has been called the second dirtiest industry in the world.

Where is apparel made?

Mouseover an individual state to see more details.

Shown above is a 2015 state-by-state breakdown of where apparel manufacturers are located in the U.S. There were a total of 6958 apparel manufacturers in 2015 (as defined by the North American Industry Classification System), which continued a fairly significant downward trend:
